# Cost

VecLabs is **88% cheaper than Pinecone** at equivalent scale. This isn't a promotional claim - it follows directly from the architecture.

---

| Savings vs Pinecone | -          | baseline    | 64% cheaper  | 64% cheaper    |

VecLabs is 99.6% cheaper than Pinecone at 1M vectors.

---

## Why the cost difference is so large

**Centralized providers charge for:**

- Server infrastructure (EC2, GKE)
- Network bandwidth
- Corporate margin (typically 70-80% on cloud services)
- Engineering and support overhead
- Investor return expectations

**VecLabs charges for:**

- Shadow Drive storage: ~$0.05/GB/year (near raw disk cost)
- Solana transactions: $0.00025/write (fixed by protocol)
- VecLabs platform fee: $0 during alpha

The decentralized architecture removes the middleman margin entirely.
